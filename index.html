Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

MyMojo does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app connects to MPD servers on your local network to control playback.

Local network access is used only to discover and connect to your MPD device. MyMojo does not send your data to external servers and does not include analytics or advertising SDKs.

If you contact support by email, we use your message only to respond to your request. You can request deletion by emailing support@keefit.com.
